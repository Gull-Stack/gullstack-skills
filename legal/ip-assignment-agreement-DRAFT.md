**4. Client contracts.** Contracts listed in Exhibit C shall be assigned only upon any required counterparty notice or consent. Until assignment of a given contract, Assignor shall hold it for the benefit of Assignee and remit associated net revenue to Assignee, to the extent permitted by the contract. *(⚠️ Counsel to review: ongoing fee-for-service client work inside the 501(c)(3) raises unrelated-business-income and private-benefit issues — the parties may instead terminate, novate to a taxable subsidiary, or restructure these engagements.)*

Further assurances.** Assignor shall execute all documents and take all actions reasonably requested by Assignee to perfect the assignments, including registrar domain transfers, GitHub organization/repository ownership transfers, and platform account transfers (Vercel, SendGrid, Meta Business Manager, Google Analytics).

**6. Representations.** Assignor represents that it owns the Assigned Assets free of liens and encumbrances, has full power to make this assignment, and that no third party's consent is required except as listed in Exhibit C.

**7. Miscellaneous.** This Agreement is governed by Utah law; it is the entire agreement of the parties regarding its subject matter; it may be executed in counterparts.
