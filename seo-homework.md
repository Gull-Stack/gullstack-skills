# SEO Best Practices 2026 — My Homework

## The Top 8 Ranking Factors (Priority Order)

### 1. **Quality Content**
- Most important factor in 2026
- Content must be helpful, informative, and relevant
- Depth matters: first page results average ~1400 words
- Cover topics comprehensively, not superficially
- Original content — no scraping or thin content

### 2. **E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)**
- Google's way to combat AI-generated garbage
- It's essentially **social proof** — what OTHERS say about you matters more than what you say about yourself
- Build through: podcasts, interviews, reviews on third-party sites, mentions, case studies
- Smaller blogs with real lived experience beating faceless corporate blogs

### 3. **Backlinks**
- Still a core ranking signal
- Quality over quantity — links from authoritative, relevant sites
- Act as "votes of confidence"

### 4. **Technical SEO**
- Page speed (Core Web Vitals)
- Mobile-friendliness (mobile-first indexing)
- Crawlability and clean indexing
- XML sitemap accuracy
- URL structure
- Schema markup / structured data
- No broken links
- Proper HTML / W3C validation

### 5. **Keyword Optimization**
- Keywords still matter
- Keyword in title tag (front-loaded performs better)
- Keyword in H1, H2, H3 tags
- Keyword in first 100 words
- LSI (related) keywords for context
- Don't over-optimize — natural usage

### 6. **User Experience (UX)**
- How easy/enjoyable is the site to use?
- Click-through rate (CTR) impacts rankings
- Time on site, bounce rate signals
- Mobile usability critical
- Hidden content on mobile may not be indexed

### 7. **Brand Signals**
- Branded search volume correlates with overall rankings
- Get people searching "{brand} + {target keyword}"
- Strong brand = tide that lifts all boats

### 8. **Freshness**
- Content recency matters for time-sensitive queries
- Regular updates signal active site
- Historical page update frequency matters

## 2026 Specific Trends

### Branded Search Tactics
- Growing branded search volume helps all keywords
- Manufacture word of mouth: social content, influencer, PR
- Get people to search "ATSports pickleball court paint" → helps rank for "pickleball court paint"

### Clickbait Titles with Personality
- First-person pronouns increase CTR
- "I tested 10 tools for 6 months" beats "10 Best Tools 2026"
- Show personality, make promises you deliver on
- Higher CTR = better rankings

### YouTube + Written Content
- Video converts better than written
- Embed YouTube videos in blog posts
- Builds trust and authority
- Helps combat AI Overview threat

### Human Content > AI Content
- Google rewarding human-written content with real experience
- AI-generated content being devalued
- Authenticity and personality win

## Local SEO (For businesses like ATSports)

### Google Business Profile (GMB)
- #1 factor for local pack rankings (32%)
- Must be: claimed, verified, updated
- Relevant local content
- Regular posts (weekly)
- Photos, Q&A, reviews all matter
- Response rate to reviews matters

### Citations
- Presence on "best of" lists
- Quality/authority of unstructured citations (newspaper articles, blogs, industry associations)
- NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone) across web

## On-Page SEO Checklist

1. **Title Tag** — Keyword at front, compelling, under 60 chars
2. **Meta Description** — Not a ranking factor but affects CTR
3. **H1 Tag** — One per page, contains keyword
4. **H2/H3 Structure** — Logical hierarchy, keywords where natural
5. **URL** — Clean, keyword-rich, short
6. **Internal Links** — Link to important pages, use descriptive anchors
7. **Outbound Links** — Link to authoritative sources (builds trust)
8. **Images** — Optimized file names, alt text, compressed
9. **Schema Markup** — Help Google understand content type
10. **Mobile Optimization** — Test on real devices
11. **Page Speed** — Target <3s load time
12. **Content Length** — Match or exceed competitors for the keyword
